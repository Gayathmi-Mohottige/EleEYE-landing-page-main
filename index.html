Our Features
EleEYE uses AI-powered detection and real-time community updates to prevent elephant-vehicle collisions. Stay informed with instant alerts, navigate high-risk areas safely, and help protect both lives and wildlife one alert at a time.
Real-Time Detection
Our AI-powered system continuously scans for elephants near roadways, providing instant location updates to help drivers and communities react quickly.
Community Reporting
Empowering users to report and verify elephant sightings, ensuring real-time accuracy and enhancing safety through crowd-sourced data.
Automatic Warnings
Smart warning lights activate on-site when an elephant is detected nearby, alerting drivers and reducing the risk of collisions.
Mobile Notifications
Receive instant alerts on your phone when you're approaching an elephant zone, allowing you to take precautionary measures in advance.
Offline Support
No internet? No problem! EleEYE stores critical updates so you can access recent elephant sightings even in remote areas with low connectivity.
24/7 Monitoring
AI-powered surveillance works round the clock, ensuring continuous tracking of elephant movements and improving road safety at all times.
